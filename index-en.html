Developmental advantages

Modern high-tech toys are designed to entertain and attract childrens attention, but are often very loud and overstimulative. Wooden toys tend to encourage children to engage in creative and imaginative play.

Enabling them to participate in meaningful games, develop cognitive skills and problem solving skills that will be used throughout the rest of their life.

Educational wooden toys are an important tool in a childs development. Many educational wooden toys build lateral critical thinking and problem-solving skills and help build motor skills.

The atmosphere created around the child in an environment with natural toys is a quiet, peaceful and ultimately nicer one than the atmosphere in a room with plastic toys running on batteries.

Wooden toys are much safer. They are a completely natural alternative to plastic toys because they do not contain high levels of chemicals and toxins. This is especially important for young children who tend to put everything in their mouth.

The perfect gift for our youngest

Cubes are attractive to children from an early age. In the first year of his life the child will observe the dice, roll it around in his little hands, put it in his mouth, smell it. In the second year of a childs life cubes can be used in the process of learning how to use various objects and to master a different range of colors. Clear and conspicuous letters on Ponge cubes, will be a real pleasure for a four-year old.

Since Ponge cubes are made of wood and have a scent of nature to them, they may also have the ability of reminding parents themselves of playful moments spent in nature during their own childhood. This in turn may encourage them to play along with their child, whether by constructing and tearing down cube towers, building car garages, playing word games such as "Hangman" or "Jumble", as well as to mutually think up imaginative stories.
